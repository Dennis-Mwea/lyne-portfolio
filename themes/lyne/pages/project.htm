Project Name

Website

Purple Hut is the first fee full healthy delivery website within Nairobi

Website Overview

This project is about a fast food delivery restaurant which is getting in delivery business for its customers who can order online and want the food within 25 min at their residence.

The purpose of our website is to show how we can optimize this process as much as possible,and make is not only convenient but also with a beautiful and clean UI.

The website makes ordering process smooth and quick, without the customers lining up and waiting in long queues to order.It also helps make pre order from away with predefined time schedule.
